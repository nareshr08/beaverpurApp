We are who we are!!

We are a small but growing Tennis community based in Beaverton, Hillsboro & Portland, Oregon. Every summer for the past few years we have been coming together to play some competitive tennis while having fun and exercise.

Adult's Singles, Doubles and Team tennis are the 3 tournaments we conduct.

Use the Contact link to get in touch with us.

If you like, subscribe to our Google Group & Facebook page and introduce yourself.
